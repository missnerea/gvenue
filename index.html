Enclosed garden weddings

Garden wedding venues can infuse your day with a whimsical, fairytale-like quality that can be achieved or enhanced by personal styling.

Outside Ceremonies

For some wedding guests venue changes can be quite an inconvenience and nobody enjoys a time-break in between, this simply interferes with the flow and energy of the day. An outside garden venue can cater for both the ceremony and the reception with equal levels of quality maintained throughout, from excellent reception and dining areas to quality lighting, sound and dance floors.

Outside Wedding Reception

An outside reception lends itself to the possibility of multiple ceremony locations. We all like to work with options and a garden setting gives you just that, and often includes the choice of an outdoor or indoor ceremony area perfect for those who are a little weather weary.
